Purchase Analysis

This is Personal Project focused on identifying the pattern of purchase from different viewpoint and factors affecting the purchase pattern of customers which impacts the business.
Data Source: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/sanyamgoyal401/customer-purchases-behaviour-dataset 
Tools used: Microsoft Excel 
Analysis: Descriptive Analysis, Prescriptive Analysis.
_________________________________

List of Columns: (We have created custom columns with categorial data based on numerical data for better analysis)

Customer ID: This is customer unique identifier recorded by business entity Age: Here, customer age is recorded Gender: Here, Customer gender has been recorded Income: This is numerical figure of what they earn every month Education: Here, we record qualification of our customer Region: Here, we record the belonging of customer Loyalty Status: Here, Customer has been categorized as Gold, Silver & Regular based on their Loyalty Purchase Frequency: This has been categorized as how frequent customers are coming to purchase. Classified as Rare, Occasional & Frequent Purchase Amount: This is numerical figure of what they Spend every month Product Category: Here, we record category they purchased Promotion Usage: Here, we record if promotion was used or not Satisfaction Score: This is Numerical figure where we ask our customer to rate their satisfaction Satisfaction Category: Here, we have categorized Score as Promoter (9-10), Passive (7-8) & Detractor (Below 7) Age Category: Here, we have categorized age as if below 18 is known as teen, 18-25 is Young Adult, 25-40 is Adult, 40-60 is Senior Adult, more than 60 are Old Age

Problems Statements:

What kind of customers are purchasing more based on region, and what does the business need to understand about their purchasing patterns?
What is the highest type of purchase?
What is the customer satisfaction score based on NPS (Net Promoter Score) metrics for purchases made so far?
Which products have the highest purchase frequency compared to others?
__________________________________________

Finding:

East & West region has higher purchases and value of those purchases are medium.
Medium Valued purchases are highest contributor in overall purchases following that low value purchases.
NPS so far is -93% which is very low.
Electronics, Clothing, Books, Food has highest purchase frequency compared to others.
Proposed Solution: We must analyse the sentiment of customer to identify the cause of such low NPS scores. We must propose offers on electronics, Clothing, Books, Food which has higher frequency for maximum profits We must re-evaluate the process of assisting customer or whom so ever is interacting with customer for better understanding on ground level In East and West region, we have good rate of purchases so we must evaluate to understand what drives purchase in these areas and do same in other regions with better customer experience Frequent customer has rated 5 which have major contribution so we must focus on this customer for our retention process Customer are preferring to spend medium value (between 8000-15000) in their purchases. So, any products in this range is high likely to be sold. Loyalty campaign needs to evaluate as our gold and silver customers have low purchase amount and huge contribution in Detractors (There are almost 0.00% detractors from gold status)
